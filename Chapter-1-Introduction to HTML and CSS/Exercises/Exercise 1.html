Macaroni and Cheese

This is a simple and classic recipe for a delicious macaroni and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Stir in flour until smooth, cooking for about 1 minute.
  4. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly, until mixture is thickened.
  5. Stir in shredded cheddar cheese until melted and sauce is smooth.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Pour cheese sauce over cooked macaroni and stir until well coated.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
